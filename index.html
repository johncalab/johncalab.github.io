About me

I am currently a software engineer at facebook, working on a distributed timeseries database for realtime monitoring. In a previous life I used to think about pure math research.

Short Bio

I grew up in Italy, studied math at the university of Pisa, and later earned my PhD from the university of Oxford (technically, it's called a DPhil). Broadly speaking, my research was in algebraic geometry. My advisor was Tom Bridgeland.
For a about a year I was a Doctoral Prize Fellow at Imperial College London, and afterwards I spent a few years at Rice university as an Instructor and NSF Research Fellow. At the end of my time at Rice, I was lucky enough to receive offers to be a tenure-track professor, but decided to switch fields.* I was briefly a Research Investigator at MD Anderson Cancer Center, then a Senior Data Scientist at Walmart, and now a Software Engineer at Facebook.

Here is an (outdated) academic CV.
*Oddly enough, I now work longer hours but I am overall much happier.
